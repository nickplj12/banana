After global warming has melted all the glaciers, humanity has found refuge near the poles, in a newly formed ocean. The only food they remembered to take with them was bananas, so they started planting. They have recently have found ways to genetically engineer more bananas, so there is no need for banana trees. In the banana archipelago, there are a few islands. Banana Island, Violence Island, Wooper Island, and some smaller unnamed islands.Explore the experimental weaponry on Violence Island, but be careful, because the effects of prolonged exposure to the weapons is not a part of this test. Be careful when operating the banana factory, as one wrong move could cause humanity to go extinct due to global warming. You should also refrain from pressing the "obama prism respawn" button too many times, as that could cause an interdimensional rift that would likely cause the end of the world. But the most important thing to remember is that violence on Banana Island will get you banned.
